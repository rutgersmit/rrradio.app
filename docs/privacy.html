Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Rrradio is a simple radio streaming app for Mac. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

Rrradio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Network requests

Rrradio makes the following network requests, all initiated by you:

  • Radio streams — to play the station you select.
  • Station catalog — to fetch the list of default stations on first launch.
  • Album artwork — to look up cover art for the currently playing track via the iTunes Search API. Only the song title is sent, not any personal information.

Local storage

Your station list and preferences are stored locally on your device using macOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your Mac.
